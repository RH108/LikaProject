Politics

Trump's "Big, Beautiful Bill" Faces House Hurdle After Senate Pass

*Washington D.C.*

President Donald Trump's controversial "One Big Beautiful Bill Act" has narrowly passed the Republican-controlled Senate, sending it to the House of Representatives for a critical vote. The bill, which seeks to make permanent the 2017 tax cuts and introduce new tax breaks while also implementing deep spending cuts, has added an estimated $3.3 trillion to the national debt, drawing fierce criticism from opponents. Debates are expected to be heated in the House, where the outcome remains uncertain.

EU Nations Miss Climate Deadline, Funds for Vulnerable at Risk

*Brussels*

European Union countries have reportedly missed a crucial climate deadline, potentially jeopardizing funds designated for vulnerable populations. The missed target comes amidst a scorching heatwave across Europe, with several major landmarks, including the Eiffel Tower and Brussels' Atomium, closing due to extreme temperatures. The development raises concerns about the EU's commitment to its climate goals and its ability to protect its citizens from the escalating impacts of global warming.

Thailand's PM Suspended Amidst Leaked Phone Call Probe

*Bangkok*

Thai Prime Minister Paetongtarn Shinawatra has been suspended by the Constitutional Court pending an investigation into a leaked phone call. The decision comes amidst broader legal challenges for the Shinawatra family, with former Prime Minister Thaksin Shinawatra also facing a separate court case. The political uncertainty has gripped Thailand, with observers closely watching developments.

Defence

Romania Secures $2.23 Billion V/SHORAD Defense Deal with Rafael

*Bucharest*

Romania has finalized a significant $2.23 billion deal with Israel's Rafael Advanced Defense Systems for its Very Short and Short Range Air Defense (V/SHORAD) acquisition program. The agreement marks a major boost to Romania's defensive capabilities, strengthening its air defense systems amidst evolving regional security concerns.

US Air Force Axes E-7 Wedgetail Program, A-10 Retirement Planned

*Washington D.C.*

The US Air Force has announced the termination of its E-7 Wedgetail early warning aircraft program, signaling a shift in its aerial defense strategy. Furthermore, the Air Force plans to retire its entire A-10 Warthog fleet, among other older aircraft, as part of a broader effort to free up funds for the development of future aviation technologies. The move is expected to face scrutiny from some lawmakers and military analysts.

Ukrainian Drone Strikes Deep Inside Russia After Record Russian Attacks

*Kyiv/Moscow*

A Ukrainian drone has reportedly struck a plant deep inside Russian territory, following what Ukraine describes as a record number of Russian drone attacks in June. The incident highlights the escalating nature of the conflict, with both sides employing long-range drone capabilities. Meanwhile, Russia claims to have occupied all of Ukraine's Luhansk region, a claim yet to be independently verified.

Lifestyle

Inflammaging: Lifestyle Factor, Not Universal Aging Trait, Study Suggests

*New York*

A new study from Columbia University suggests that "inflammaging," previously considered a universal hallmark of aging, may instead be largely influenced by lifestyle factors. The research indicates that chronic inflammation, often associated with aging, might not be an inevitable part of the human experience and could be mitigated through healthier choices, offering new insights into longevity and well-being.

Olivia Munn Reveals Battle with Trichotillomania

*Los Angeles*

Actress Olivia Munn has publicly shared her struggle with Trichotillomania, a disorder characterized by the compulsive urge to pull out one's hair. Munn linked her condition to a past paparazzi incident involving Chris Pine, shedding light on the personal impact of public scrutiny and the importance of mental health awareness.

The unconventional design, which blends Indian street culture with high fashion, has garnered significant attention online, sparking conversations about cultural inspiration and luxury trends.
